About Children

Orphaned and homeless children crave for the unconditional love, kindness, and generosity of people around them. They need a family that supports them through thick and thin; they need YOU to cushion them from all the adverse hardships life brings into their way.

At Helping Hands, we find homeless and abandoned kids who have survived incredible poverty, adversity, exploitation, and negligence. Most of the times, infants are discarded like trash in putrid piles of broken bottles, disposed of sanitary items and rubbish—left at the mercy of wild, stray animals. At times, we come across kids who are abused by their own blood relations. We provide them security, shelter, medical treatment, and legal protection against the culprits.

As ironic as it sounds, adoption is not much appreciated in our society. The adoptive kids and parents both encounter a multitude of issues in socializing and discrimination every step of their way. However, it makes our heart happy and our eyes wet, when we see a homeless kid get home, he or she can call his or her own.

Kids waiting for a family usually live with a shelter home family, where they get a nurturing, family-like atmosphere. We do everything in our power for assuring that the child you are going to adopt has whatever he or she might need to thrive. Since we ensure transparency across our processes, we provide parents with the most accurate information about their future child’s growth, medical conditions, and background.

Whether you live in Pakistan or anywhere around the world and are interested in adopting a child from our orphanage, Sarim Burney Welfare Trust International will efficiently help you through the child-selection process. We believe that adopting one child won’t change the world; but for that child, the world will change. Just take the first step, the rest will come easier.
